# 顾问报告契约

## Header

State season, ruleset/build, fixed BD, analyzed slot, and confidence.

## Equipment comparison

Use a table with A, B, absolute delta, and percentage delta for:

- steady expected single hit;
- full-buff theoretical single hit;
- expected damage per cast;
- sustained DPS when inputs support it;
- physical and elemental EHP.

Then list the three largest factor changes, breakpoint changes, survival tradeoff, recommendation, and conditions that would reverse it.

## Maximum hit

Report expected largest single event and theoretical ceiling as separate numbers. Also show per-cast expectation, the selected event, active conditions, and excluded repeated hits.

## Panel diagnosis

Report the top three issues in priority order. For each, state current value, target/cap or breakpoint, exact gap/overcap, expected improvement, and a concrete temper/masterworking action. Finish with damage-first and high-tier-survival paths.

## Missing information

Ask for the smallest specific screenshot or field set that can resolve the decision. Do not ask for the whole character again.

## Integrity

Label model-exact values, calibrated estimates, and unknown mechanics separately. Never hide a range behind excess decimal places.
